# Blog Post Template

## Structure

```markdown
# [Title — under 60 characters, includes primary keyword]

**Meta description:** [150–155 characters, includes primary keyword and CTA]

[Opening hook — 2–3 sentences that identify the reader's pain point or curiosity. State what they'll learn.]

## [H2 section — context or background]

[Set the scene. Why does this matter now? Include a supporting statistic.]

## [H2 section — the core insight or method]

[The main value of the article. Break into H3 subsections if needed.]

### [H3 subsection]

[Detail, example, or step]

### [H3 subsection]

[Detail, example, or step]

## [H2 section — practical application]

[How to apply this. Numbered steps, checklist, or framework.]

1. **Step one** — [description]
2. **Step two** — [description]
3. **Step three** — [description]

## [H2 section — results or evidence]

[Case study, data, or testimonial supporting the approach.]

## What to do next

[Summarise the key takeaway in one to two sentences. Clear CTA.]

[CTA button text or link]
```

## Checklist

- [ ] Title under 60 characters
- [ ] Primary keyword in title, first paragraph, and at least one H2
- [ ] Meta description 150–155 characters with CTA
- [ ] Opening hook addresses reader's pain point
- [ ] At least one statistic with source
- [ ] Scannable: headings, bullets, short paragraphs
- [ ] CTA aligned with brief objectives
- [ ] Word count within target range
- [ ] British English spelling throughout
- [ ] Images have alt text suggestions
- [ ] No heading level skips (H1 → H2 → H3)
