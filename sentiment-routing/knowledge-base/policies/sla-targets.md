# SLA Targets

## First Response Time

| Priority | Target | Measurement |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| P1 — Critical | 15 minutes | From ticket creation to first human response |
| P2 — High | 1 hour | From ticket creation to first human response |
| P3 — Medium | 4 hours | From ticket creation to first human response |
| P4 — Low | 24 hours | From ticket creation to first human response |

## Resolution Time

| Priority | Target | Measurement |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| P1 — Critical | 1 hour | From ticket creation to confirmed resolution |
| P2 — High | 8 hours | From ticket creation to confirmed resolution |
| P3 — Medium | 24 hours | From ticket creation to confirmed resolution |
| P4 — Low | 72 hours | From ticket creation to confirmed resolution |

## Business Hours

- SLA clocks run during business hours only (Mon-Fri 08:00-20:00 GMT) **except** for P1 tickets, which run 24/7
- Saturday hours (09:00-17:00 GMT) count for P1 and P2 only
- Bank holidays are excluded except for P1

## Breach Procedures

### Warning (75% of SLA elapsed)
- Automatic notification to assigned agent
- Ticket highlighted in queue

### Breach (100% of SLA elapsed)
- Automatic escalation to team lead
- Incident logged for SLA reporting
- Customer receives proactive update if no response sent

### Critical Breach (150% of SLA elapsed)
- Escalation to department manager
- Mandatory root cause analysis within 24 hours
- Customer receives apology and expedited handling

## VIP SLA Adjustments

VIP customers receive one priority level upgrade for SLA purposes:
- P2 ticket → P1 SLA timelines
- P3 ticket → P2 SLA timelines
- P4 ticket → P3 SLA timelines

## Reporting

SLA compliance is reported weekly with targets:
- P1: 95% compliance
- P2: 90% compliance
- P3: 85% compliance
- P4: 80% compliance
