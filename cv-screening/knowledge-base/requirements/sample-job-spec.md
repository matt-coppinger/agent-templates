# Senior Software Engineer — Job Specification

## About the Role

We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our Platform team. You'll design, build, and maintain the core services that power our product, working closely with product engineers and infrastructure teams.

## Location

London, UK (hybrid — 3 days in office)

## Must-Have Requirements

- **Experience**: 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
- **Python**: Strong proficiency in Python (our primary backend language)
- **Cloud platforms**: Hands-on experience with AWS (EC2, S3, Lambda, RDS at minimum)
- **Databases**: Experience designing and optimising relational databases (PostgreSQL preferred)
- **API design**: Experience building and maintaining RESTful APIs
- **Testing**: Strong testing practices (unit, integration, end-to-end)
- **Version control**: Proficient with Git and collaborative development workflows
- **Communication**: Ability to communicate technical concepts clearly to non-technical stakeholders

## Nice-to-Have Requirements

- **Container orchestration**: Experience with Kubernetes or similar
- **Infrastructure as Code**: Terraform, CloudFormation, or Pulumi
- **GraphQL**: Experience building or consuming GraphQL APIs
- **Event-driven architecture**: Kafka, RabbitMQ, or similar message brokers
- **Mentoring**: Track record of mentoring junior engineers
- **Open source**: Contributions to open source projects
- **CI/CD**: Experience designing deployment pipelines

## Experience Range

- Minimum: 5 years
- Maximum: 10 years
- Sweet spot: 6–8 years

## What You'll Do

- Design and implement new platform services
- Improve reliability and performance of existing systems
- Participate in architecture decisions and technical design reviews
- Mentor mid-level and junior engineers
- Contribute to engineering culture and best practices

## Salary Range

£75,000–£95,000 depending on experience

## Benefits

- 25 days annual leave + bank holidays
- Private health insurance
- Pension contribution (5% employer match)
- Learning & development budget (£1,500/year)
- Cycle to work scheme
