# Compliance Calendar — Key Regulatory Dates

## Recurring Annual Deadlines

### April
- **National Minimum Wage / National Living Wage rate changes** — typically effective 1 April
- **Tax year start** — 6 April (PAYE, NIC adjustments)
- **Gender pay gap reporting deadline** — 4 April (private sector), 30 March (public sector)
- **Statutory payment rate changes** — SSP, SMP, SPP, SAP updated

### May
- **Modern Slavery Statement deadline** — within 6 months of financial year-end (many organisations have 31 March year-end → due by 30 September, but some by end of May)

### June
- **HSE annual statistics publication** — typically late June/early July

### July
- **ICO annual report** — typically published July
- **FCA annual report** — typically July

### September
- **Modern Slavery Statements** — deadline for 31 March year-end companies

### October
- **Employment law changes** — many come into force 1 October or 6 April

### December
- **FCA regulatory fees** — typically due by end of year

## Quarterly
- **HMRC RTI submissions** — ongoing with each pay run
- **Pension contributions** — auto-enrolment ongoing compliance
- **ICO registration renewal** — annual but staggered by registration date

## Monitoring Guidance

- Check regulatory websites **weekly** for updates to this calendar
- Set alerts for **30 days** before each deadline
- Review calendar at the start of each quarter
- Flag any new legislation with phased implementation dates
