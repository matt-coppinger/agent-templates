|-------------------|---------------|------------------|

| Employment Law | HR / People | Legal, Finance (payroll), All managers |

## Department Responsibilities

### Legal / Compliance
- Overall regulatory monitoring coordination
- Contract and policy review
- Regulatory relationship management
- Escalation point for complex compliance questions

### Data Protection / Privacy
- ICO registration and liaison
- Data protection impact assessments (DPIAs)
- Subject access requests
- Breach notification
- Third-party data processing agreements

### HR / People
- Employment contracts and policies
- Equality and diversity compliance
- Right to work checks
- Working time compliance
- Family leave administration
- Pay and benefits compliance (NMW, gender pay gap)

### Finance
- Financial regulatory compliance (if applicable)
- Tax compliance (PAYE, NIC)
- Anti-money laundering (if applicable)
- Pension auto-enrolment
- Modern slavery statement (often finance-led for reporting)

### IT / Engineering
- Information security controls
- System access and data handling
- AI/automated decision-making systems
- Cyber security compliance

### Health & Safety / Facilities
- Risk assessments
- RIDDOR reporting
- Fire safety
- Workplace conditions
- COSHH management
- First aid provision

### Operations
- Supply chain compliance
- Environmental reporting
- Waste management
- Business continuity

### Board / Senior Leadership
- Corporate governance
- Corporate manslaughter liability
- Strategic compliance decisions
- Regulatory budget allocation

## Cross-Functional Changes

When a regulatory change affects 3+ departments:
1. Assign a **lead department** (usually the primary domain owner)
2. Establish a **working group** with representatives from each affected department
3. Report progress to **Board / Senior Leadership**
4. Ensure **Legal / Compliance** provides oversight
