# Commercial Due Diligence Checklist

## Market and Industry
- [ ] Market size and growth analysis
- [ ] Competitive landscape and positioning
- [ ] Industry regulatory trends
- [ ] Barriers to entry analysis
- [ ] SWOT analysis or equivalent

## Revenue and Customers
- [ ] Revenue breakdown by customer (top 20)
- [ ] Revenue breakdown by product/service
- [ ] Revenue breakdown by geography
- [ ] Customer concentration analysis
- [ ] Customer churn rates (last 3 years)
- [ ] Customer satisfaction data (NPS, surveys)
- [ ] Sales pipeline and backlog
- [ ] Pricing strategy documentation

## Contracts and Relationships
- [ ] Key customer contracts (terms, duration, renewal)
- [ ] Customer dependency analysis (any >10% of revenue)
- [ ] Long-term contracts vs recurring vs one-off revenue
- [ ] Contract renewal history and win rates

## Supply Chain
- [ ] Key supplier contracts and terms
- [ ] Supplier concentration analysis
- [ ] Alternative supplier assessment
- [ ] Supply chain risk assessment
- [ ] Raw material pricing trends (if applicable)

## Products and Services
- [ ] Product/service portfolio overview
- [ ] Product roadmap
- [ ] R&D pipeline and investment
- [ ] Product lifecycle analysis
- [ ] Margin analysis by product/service

## Sales and Marketing
- [ ] Sales team structure and performance
- [ ] Marketing strategy and spend
- [ ] Customer acquisition cost analysis
- [ ] Channel strategy
- [ ] Brand strength assessment

## Operations
- [ ] Operational capacity and utilisation
- [ ] Quality management systems
- [ ] Key operational metrics and KPIs
- [ ] Business continuity plans
- [ ] Technology infrastructure overview
