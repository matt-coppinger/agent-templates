# Financial Due Diligence Checklist

## Statutory Accounts
- [ ] Audited accounts (last 3 years)
- [ ] Audit management letters (last 3 years)
- [ ] Accounting policies summary

## Management Accounts
- [ ] Monthly management accounts (last 12 months)
- [ ] Budget vs actual variance analysis
- [ ] Board packs with financial commentary

## Forecasts and Projections
- [ ] Financial model / business plan
- [ ] Revenue forecasts with assumptions
- [ ] Cash flow projections (12 months forward)

## Tax
- [ ] Corporation tax computations (last 3 years)
- [ ] Corporation tax returns (last 3 years)
- [ ] VAT returns (last 12 months)
- [ ] PAYE/NIC compliance records
- [ ] Any HMRC correspondence or enquiries
- [ ] Transfer pricing documentation (if applicable)
- [ ] R&D tax credit claims

## Working Capital
- [ ] Debtor ageing report (current)
- [ ] Creditor ageing report (current)
- [ ] Bad debt provisions and write-off history
- [ ] Stock/inventory valuation and methodology
- [ ] Working capital normalisation analysis

## Debt and Finance
- [ ] Bank facility agreements
- [ ] Loan agreements and schedules
- [ ] Hire purchase and leasing agreements
- [ ] Intercompany loan agreements
- [ ] Bank statements (last 6 months)
- [ ] Guarantees given or received

## Capital Expenditure
- [ ] Fixed asset register
- [ ] Capital expenditure plans
- [ ] Depreciation schedules and policies

## Related Party Transactions
- [ ] Schedule of related party transactions
- [ ] Management charges / intercompany agreements
- [ ] Director loan accounts

## Other
- [ ] Grant agreements and conditions
- [ ] Government subsidies received
- [ ] Contingent liabilities schedule
- [ ] Off-balance-sheet arrangements
