# Legal Due Diligence Checklist

## Corporate Structure
- [ ] Certificate of incorporation
- [ ] Memorandum and articles of association (current)
- [ ] Shareholders' agreement
- [ ] Share register and share certificates
- [ ] Corporate structure chart (group organogram)
- [ ] Board minutes (last 3 years)
- [ ] Shareholder resolutions (last 3 years)
- [ ] Companies House filings (confirmation statements, annual returns)

## Material Contracts
- [ ] Schedule of all material contracts (value >Â£50k or strategic importance)
- [ ] Key customer contracts (top 10 by revenue)
- [ ] Key supplier contracts (top 10 by spend)
- [ ] Joint venture or partnership agreements
- [ ] Distribution or agency agreements
- [ ] Franchise agreements

## Change of Control
- [ ] Contracts with change of control provisions
- [ ] Consents required on change of ownership
- [ ] Pre-emption rights affecting the transaction

## Litigation and Disputes
- [ ] Schedule of pending litigation
- [ ] Schedule of threatened claims
- [ ] Historical litigation (last 5 years)
- [ ] Regulatory proceedings or investigations
- [ ] Alternative dispute resolution proceedings
- [ ] Settlement agreements

## Regulatory and Compliance
- [ ] Licences and permits (schedule and copies)
- [ ] Regulatory approvals and conditions
- [ ] Compliance policies and procedures
- [ ] Anti-bribery and corruption policy
- [ ] Anti-money laundering procedures
- [ ] Sanctions screening procedures
- [ ] Data protection registration and policies (GDPR)
- [ ] Health and safety policy and records

## Real Property
- [ ] Schedule of properties (freehold and leasehold)
- [ ] Title deeds / Land Registry entries
- [ ] Lease agreements
- [ ] Rent review documentation
- [ ] Planning permissions and building regulations
- [ ] Environmental reports and surveys
- [ ] Asbestos surveys

## Insurance
- [ ] Schedule of insurance policies
- [ ] Current policy documents
- [ ] Claims history (last 5 years)
- [ ] Pending claims

## Intellectual Property
- [ ] IP register (patents, trademarks, designs, copyrights)
- [ ] IP registration certificates
- [ ] IP licence agreements (in and out)
- [ ] Domain name register
- [ ] Trade secret protection measures
- [ ] IP infringement claims (active or historical)

## Data Protection
- [ ] Data protection impact assessments
- [ ] Privacy notices
- [ ] Data processing agreements
- [ ] Records of processing activities
- [ ] ICO correspondence
- [ ] Data breach register
