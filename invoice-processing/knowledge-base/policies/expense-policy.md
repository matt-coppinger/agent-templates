# Company Expense Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy sets out the rules for submitting, approving, and reimbursing business expenses and processing supplier invoices.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all employees, contractors, and departments submitting expenses or processing invoices on behalf of the company.

## 3. General Rules

### 3.1 Receipts
- All expenses over £25 must have an original receipt or invoice attached
- Credit card statements alone are not sufficient proof of purchase
- Digital receipts (PDF, email) are acceptable

### 3.2 Timeliness
- Expense claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being incurred
- Invoices must be submitted for processing within 7 days of receipt

### 3.3 Pre-Approval
- Any single expense over £1,000 requires pre-approval from a Department Head
- Any commitment over £5,000 requires Finance Director pre-approval
- Capital expenditure over £10,000 requires CFO approval

## 4. Spending Limits by Category

| Category | Per-Item Limit | Monthly Limit |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| Travel (rail/air) | £500 | £2,000 |
| Accommodation | £200/night | £1,000 |
| Meals & entertainment | £75/head | £500 |
| Office supplies | £250 | £1,000 |
| Software licences | £500 | £2,000 |
| Professional services | £5,000 | £20,000 |
| Equipment | £2,000 | £10,000 |
| Training | £2,000 | £5,000 |

## 5. Approval Thresholds

| Amount | Approver |
|--------|----------|
| Up to £500 | Line Manager |
| £501 – £2,500 | Department Head |
| £2,501 – £10,000 | Finance Director |
| £10,001 – £50,000 | CFO |
| Over £50,000 | Board |

## 6. Travel

- Economy class for all flights under 4 hours
- Business class permitted for flights over 4 hours with Department Head approval
- Standard class rail travel; first class only with pre-approval
- Car mileage reimbursed at HMRC approved rate (currently 45p/mile for first 10,000 miles)

## 7. Meals & Entertainment

- Client entertainment must have business justification documented
- Alcohol is permitted in moderation for client entertainment only
- Team meals limited to £30/head without pre-approval

## 8. Supplier Invoices

- All invoices must reference a valid purchase order number
- Invoices without PO numbers require Department Head authorisation
- Payment terms: standard Net 30 unless otherwise agreed
- Early payment discounts should be taken where beneficial

## 9. VAT

- VAT receipts are required for all VAT-recoverable expenses
- Ensure supplier VAT numbers are valid before processing
- Zero-rated and exempt items must be coded correctly

## 10. Non-Compliant Expenses

- Expenses that do not comply with this policy may be rejected
- Repeated non-compliance will be referred to the employee's line manager
- Fraudulent claims will result in disciplinary action
