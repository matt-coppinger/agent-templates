## Parties

This Agreement is entered into between:

**Party A:** [Company Name], a company registered in England and Wales (Company No. [NUMBER]), whose registered office is at [ADDRESS] ("Discloser")

**Party B:** [Company Name], a company registered in England and Wales (Company No. [NUMBER]), whose registered office is at [ADDRESS] ("Recipient")

(each a "Party" and together the "Parties")

## 1. Purpose

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship ("Purpose") and in connection with this may disclose Confidential Information to each other.

## 2. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means all information (whether written, oral, visual, or electronic) disclosed by one Party to the other that:
- (a) is marked as "confidential"; or
- (b) would reasonably be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and the circumstances of disclosure.

Including but not limited to: business plans, financial data, technical information, customer lists, trade secrets, and know-how.

## 3. Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include information that:
- (a) is or becomes publicly available other than through breach of this Agreement;
- (b) was already known to the Recipient before disclosure (evidenced by written records);
- (c) is independently developed by the Recipient without reference to the Confidential Information;
- (d) is received from a third party who is not under an obligation of confidentiality; or
- (e) is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order (provided the Recipient gives prompt notice and cooperates with any protective order).

## 4. Obligations

Each Party agrees to:
- (a) keep Confidential Information strictly confidential;
- (b) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent;
- (c) use Confidential Information only for the Purpose;
- (d) limit access to employees and advisers who need to know and who are bound by obligations of confidentiality no less onerous than this Agreement;
- (e) apply the same degree of care as it applies to its own confidential information (and in any event no less than reasonable care).

## 5. Intellectual Property

No licence or right is granted under this Agreement in respect of any intellectual property. All Confidential Information remains the property of the disclosing Party.

## 6. Return of Information

Upon written request or termination of this Agreement, each Party shall:
- (a) promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information (including copies);
- (b) certify in writing that it has done so;
- (c) provided that each Party may retain copies required by law or regulation, subject to continuing confidentiality obligations.

## 7. Term and Duration

This Agreement shall remain in force for a period of **2 years** from the date of execution.

Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination for a period of **3 years**.

## 8. No Obligation to Proceed

Nothing in this Agreement obliges either Party to enter into any further agreement or proceed with the Purpose.

## 9. Remedies

Each Party acknowledges that damages may not be an adequate remedy for breach of this Agreement and that the disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition to any other remedy.

## 10. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of **England and Wales**.

The Parties submit to the **exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales**.

## 11. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties in relation to its subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions, negotiations, and agreements.

## 12. Variation

No variation of this Agreement shall be effective unless in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 13. Notices

All notices shall be in writing and sent to the addresses set out above by recorded delivery or email to the designated contact persons.
