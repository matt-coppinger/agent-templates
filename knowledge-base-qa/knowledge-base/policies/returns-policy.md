## Eligibility

- **Eligible:** First-time purchases within 30 days, billing errors, duplicate charges

## Faulty Products and Service Issues

If you experience a service outage or critical bug that prevents normal use:
- We will extend your subscription by the duration of the outage at no cost
- For outages exceeding 72 hours, you may request a pro-rated refund for the affected period
- Faulty items (hardware accessories) may be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or replacement. Contact our support team to arrange collection.

## Duplicate Charges

If you've been charged twice for the same billing period:
- Contact support@example.com immediately
- We will investigate and process a refund for the duplicate charge within 3 working days
- No additional documentation is required for duplicate charge refunds

## Cancellation

Cancelling your subscription is not the same as requesting a refund. When you cancel:
- Your access continues until the end of your current billing period
- No further charges will be made
- To also request a refund for the current period, follow the refund process above
