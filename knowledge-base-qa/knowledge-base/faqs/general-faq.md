# General FAQ

**Last updated:** 2026-02-15
**Category:** faqs

## Account & Billing

### How do I reset my password?
Visit dashboard.example.com/reset-password and enter your registered email address. You'll receive a reset link within 5 minutes. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.

### How do I change my plan?
Go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately and you'll be charged a pro-rated amount. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

### Can I get a refund?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. Contact support@example.com with your account details and reason for the refund. Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. We'll send a confirmation email. You can reactivate at any time.

## Product Features

### How many team members can I add?
This depends on your plan: Starter (1 user), Professional (up to 10), Business (up to 50), Enterprise (unlimited). You can upgrade at any time to add more users.

### Does Widget Pro work offline?
Limited offline functionality is available on the mobile app. You can view cached reports and dashboards, but editing and collaboration features require an internet connection. Changes sync automatically when you reconnect.

### Can I export my data?
Yes, go to Settings > Data > Export. You can export in CSV, JSON, or PDF format. Full data exports are available on all plans. Enterprise customers can also use the API for automated exports.

### Is my data encrypted?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We are SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. See our security page at example.com/security for full details.

## Technical Support

### How do I contact support?
- **Email:** support@example.com (all plans, 24-hour response)
- **Live chat:** Available in-app for Business and Enterprise plans (24/7)
- **Phone:** Enterprise plans only — your account manager will provide the number

### What browsers are supported?
We support the latest two versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Internet Explorer is not supported.

### The dashboard is loading slowly — what should I do?
Try these steps: (1) Clear your browser cache, (2) Disable browser extensions temporarily, (3) Check your internet speed (minimum 2 Mbps recommended), (4) Try a different browser. If the problem persists, contact support with your browser version and a screenshot.
