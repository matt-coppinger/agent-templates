# Refund Policy

## Duplicate Charges
- Full refund for any duplicate charge, no questions asked
- Refund processed within 5 business days to original payment method
- Customer notified via email when refund is processed
- No documentation required from customer for obvious duplicates

## Standard Refunds
- Refund requests within 30 days: full refund
- Refund requests 30-90 days: pro-rated refund
- Beyond 90 days: case-by-case review by billing manager

## Subscription Cancellations
- Access continues until end of current billing period
- No partial-month refunds unless service outage exceeded 48h
